ABOUT US

Head of De-Kob Media

Godfred Nyan Amening (de-kob.mobirisesite.com) is the Head of De-Kob Media where he oversees all functions of the business including engineering, product and operations. A designer at heart, Godfred is known for balancing sharp design thinking with thoughtful product strategy to create experiences that bring people together and encourage authentic communication.
Godfred has been has been engaged in social blog site for more than ten years. He was the designer for KD Media and then moved into product management where he led the design team for some years. 
Prior to KD Media, Godfred worked at Talk Tech as Network Engineer. He began his career founding a design consultancy in 2016 with office in Accra Ghana that focused on graphics, interaction and exhibition design with a friend. Godfred holds a BSc Information Technology from BlueCrest University College where he studied IT.
Born and raised in Accra, where he now lives with his family.


Founders
Harriet Akosua Hinson (Co-founder)
Harriet A.Hinson co-founded De-Kob Media and serve as CEO for 2 years. With Harriet’s focus on simplicity and inspiring creativity through solving problems with thoughtful product design, De-Kob Media is the home for innovation on visual graphics launching dozens of products.
Prior to founding De-Kob Media, Harriet is part of the startup Harriet Group of Arts. She graduated from University of Education Winneba with a BSc in Art.
